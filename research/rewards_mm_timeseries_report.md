## False-GO Guardrails

Earlier versions classified markets as GO based solely on net_edge, availability, and stability. This allowed markets with **zero tradeable depth** and **spreads of 1000–6000+ bps** to reach GO, which is misleading: the positive net_edge is real on paper but *unreachable* when no orders sit near the midpoint.

Three new hard gates now block GO:

| Gate | Threshold | Rationale |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| `avg_depth_near_mid > 0` | any USD depth ±5% of mid | Zero depth = no fills possible, reward is theoretical |
| `spread_bps_mean ≤ 300` | ≤ 300 bps | Spreads > 300 bps signal illiquid or speculative books with high adverse-selection risk |
| `hedge_cost_cv < 0.01` | CV < 1% | Hedge cost instability implies the spread-profit estimate is noisy and unreliable |

Markets that are stable and rebate-positive but fail these gates are demoted to **WATCH**: worth monitoring, but not ready for paper quoting.
